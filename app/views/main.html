Movie Rating in Different Culture

by Sean Lee and Zoo Yuan

Background ratings in different culture

There are many movie rating site on the Internet. While IMDb represents the taste of international/us audience, Douban represents the Chinese audience. Often we found that the rating of the same movie in the two sites can be very diferent. So we got all the movies info with more than 4000 ratings in both site to do some analysis (The data is snapshoted at Oct 2013). Here's what we got!
